// PROJECT OVERVIEW

Pressure Cooker is a VR cooking game where players take on the role of a food truck worker with anxiety attacks. The player must manage their stress levels while preparing and serving food to customers in a fast-paced environment.

The game features a variety of recipes, each with its own unique challenges and time constraints. Players must balance their workload, manage their stress, and keep their customers happy to succeed in the game.

// LEARNING OBJECTIVES

- Learn how to make a VR game to emphasize the importance of mental health and stress management.

// AREAS OF IMPROVEMENT

The game can be improved with better optimization and more polished mechanics to create a smoother and more enjoyable gameplay experience.
