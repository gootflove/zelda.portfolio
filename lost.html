// PROJECT OVERVIEW

Lost and Found is a video game to help foster trust between youths and police.

The player plays as a teenager who lost his wallet and goes on an adventure to find it with a police officer. During the journey, the player will come across obstacles that represent the challenges and fears that youths may have when interacting with law enforcement, such as fear of being misunderstood or treated unfairly.

// LEARNING OBJECTIVES

- Learn how to make a VR game to build trust between youths and police.

// AREAS OF IMPROVEMENT

The game could have voice over narration or animations to make the story more engaging and immersive.
