// PROJECT OVERVIEW

Our game uses the theme of Movies (Disney). The game includes popular characters from Disney movies where players are able to put their deckbuilding skills to the test.

It is a 2-5 player game that will last around 30-60 minutes per gameplay.The target audience are for ages 8 and above.

// LEARNING OBJECTIVES

- Learn how to design a card game with engaging mechanics and strategic depth.

- Create the physical cards, box, and promotional materials for the game.

// AREAS OF IMPROVEMENT

The game could be improved with more balanced mechanics and a wider variety of cards to enhance replayability and strategic options for players.
