Crashed Spaceship onto an Unknown Planet

ENVIRONMENT DESIGN GAME DEVELOPMENT

// PROJECT OVERVIEW

This is a sci-fi survival experience where the player explores a mysterious alien world after their spaceship crashes unexpectedly. Stranded with no way to contact home, the player must travel across unfamiliar environments to recover the missing spaceship parts needed to repair their ship and escape the planet.

// LEARNING OBJECTIVES

- Learn how to use the terrain tools in Unity to create large-scale outdoor environments.

- Implement interactable objects and simple quest mechanics using C# scripting in Unity.

// AREAS OF IMPROVEMENT

I could have added more variety to the environment by creating additional biomes or points of interest for the player to discover. The gameplay could also be expanded with more complex puzzles, additional enemy types, or a deeper crafting system to enhance player engagement and replayability.
