// PROJECT OVERVIEW

Budgie is a modern banking app designed to provide a seamless and secure financial management experience across multiple platforms. The app offers features such as account management, transaction tracking, and budgeting tools, all with a focus on user-friendly design and intuitive navigation.

The app also includes a calculator, spending history, and the current stock market index, providing users with comprehensive financial insights at their fingertips.

// LEARNING OBJECTIVES

Design and prototype a multi-platform application with a consistent user experience across devices using Figma.

// AREAS OF IMPROVEMENT

The app can be better optimized for performance and user engagement across different devices.
